How To Play ConnectN

You select a column, and your piece, the X, falls to the bottom of the column. Then the computer plays an O, which does the same thing.

The goal of the game is to place your pieces in a row, column, or diagonal of a certain length that depends on difficulty.

On the small board, you must get 3 in a row, 4 on a medium-sized board, and 5 on a large board.

Good luck!
